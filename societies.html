Our Chapters

Technical Societies

Specialized communities focusing on specific fields of technology to provide members with deep technical knowledge and networking opportunities.

IEEE Industry Applications Society

Formed: 19th Dec 2023

Approved by the IEEE MGA Board. Bridges the gap between theory and industry, focusing on electrical systems, industrial automation, and energy systems.

IEEE Antennas and Propagation Society

Formed: 16th Jan 2024

Approved by the IEEE MGA Board. Advances knowledge in antenna design, EM wave propagation, wireless communication, and RF systems.

IEEE Microwave Theory and Technology Society

Formed: 04th March 2026

Approved by the IEEE MGA Board. Focuses on advanced microwave engineering, RF and millimeter-wave technologies, and wireless systems.

IEEE Computer Society

Formed: 19th Feb 2026

Approved by the IEEE MGA Board. Focuses on AI, Data Science, Cybersecurity, and Software Engineering, nurtures problem-solving in IT.
