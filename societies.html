Our Chapters

Technical Societies

Specialized communities focusing on specific fields of technology to provide members with deep technical knowledge and networking opportunities.

IEEE Computer Society

Focuses on computing and information technology, organizing coding competitions, workshops on AI/ML, and technical seminars.

IEEE Women in Engineering (WIE)

Dedicated to promoting women engineers and scientists, and inspiring girls around the world to follow their academic interests in a career in engineering.

IEEE Communications Society (ComSoc)

Focuses on communications technology, networking, and wireless systems through hands-on projects and industry interactions.

IEEE Robotics and Automation Society (RAS)

A hub for robotics enthusiasts, focusing on design, automation, and intelligent systems through workshops and competitions.
