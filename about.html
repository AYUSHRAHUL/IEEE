Est. Sep 2013

Leading the future of
technical excellence

The Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers Student Branch at Centurion University of Technology and Management (CUTM), Paralakhemundi, was officially established on 30th September 2013 under IEEE Region 10 (Asia-Pacific).


Since its inception, the branch has been actively promoting technical excellence, innovation, and professional development among students. Over the years, it has served as a vibrant platform for organizing workshops, seminars, project exhibitions, and research-oriented activities, fostering a strong culture of learning and collaboration.


With a legacy of over a decade, the IEEE Student Branch at Centurion University Paralakhemundi continues to empower students to become globally competent engineers and technology leaders.

A Decade of Excellence

Our Journey

Tracing our path from a local student collective to a world-class technical branch under Region 10.

2013

Our Journey Begins

Official inception of the IEEE Student Branch at Centurion University Paralakhemundi, marking the start of a technical revolution.

2016

SCOPES 2016

Successfully hosted the first international IEEE conference SCOPES 1, establishing ourselves as a hub for engineering innovation and global research.

2023

IAS Student Branch Chapter

Expansion of our technical ecosystem with the formation of the IEEE Industry Applications Society (IAS) Student Branch Chapter.

2024

IEEE Antennas & Propagation

Empowering research in wireless communication with the official establishment of the APS Student Branch Chapter.

Dec 2024

SCOPES 2024

Continuing the legacy of excellence with the second edition of our international conference, fostering global collaboration.

2026

MTT & Computer Societies

Simultaneous launch of the Microwave Theory and Technology (MTT) and Computer Society (CS) Branch Chapters.

2027

SCOPES 2027 Upcoming

Looking ahead to the third iteration of SCOPES, aiming for even greater milestones in technical advocacy and global impact.
